20 (twenty) is the natural number following 19 and preceding 21. A group of twenty units may also be referred to as a score. 20 is a tetrahedral number as 1, 4, 10, 20. 20 is the basis for vigesimal number systems. 20 is the third composite number to be the product of a squared prime and a prime, and also the second member of the (22)q family in this form. 20 is the smallest primitive abundant number. An icosahedron has 20 faces. A dodecahedron has 20 vertices. 20 can be written as the sum of three Fibonacci numbers uniquely, i.e. 20 = 13 + 5 + 2. The product of the number of divisors and the number of proper divisors of 20 is exactly 20. 20 is the number of moves (quarter or half turns) required to optimally solve a Rubik's Cube in the worst case.
